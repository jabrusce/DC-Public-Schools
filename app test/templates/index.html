Problem Statement:
Understand and predict the performance of Washington D.C. schools.



  • Washington DC schools are graded on a STAR rating.



  • Identify independent features that impact this rating



  • School performance can be used to direct funds and identify issues

Feature Importance:




Top 5 Features:
  • Percent of Students Meeting or Exceeding Math Requirements '18-'19
  • Percent of Students Meeting or Exceeding Math Requirements '17-'18
  • Percent of Students Meeting or Exceeding ELA Requirements '18-'19
  • Percent of Students having 20+ Truant Days
  • Neighborhood Cluster


Bottom 5 Features:
  • Enrollment
  • Capacity
  • Budgeted Enrollment
  • Budgeted Amount
  • Percent of Students having 0 Truant Days
